About Yeshivat Torat Yosef

Building the next generation of passionate, learned, and proud Sephardic Jewish leaders.

Yeshivat Torat Yosef is an all-Sephardic yeshiva dedicated to helping students grow in Torah, character, and confidence.

Founded in 2022 and now thriving in Hollywood, Florida, the Yeshiva serves families from Brooklyn, Deal, South Florida, and beyond.

Every contribution supports exceptional Torah learning, strong educational standards, and a warm, community-first environment where each student is known and uplifted.
